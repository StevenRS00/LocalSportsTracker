About Local Sports Tracker

A little about us!

A Passion Project

This project was assigned to us as a kind of choose your own adventure project. We were tasked with finding an idea that we felt passionate about and coding it up. We decided to work on and code up Local Sports Tracker to help make Sports enthusiasts be able to find sporting events near them. Whether it is little league games and you want to support their teams, maybe you want to join a simple pickup game at a nearby park, or maybe you are the one wanting to host that game, Local Sports Tracker will help you be able to access all of the nearby events. That way you can have fun and enjoy life! -- The Developers
